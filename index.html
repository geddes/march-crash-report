Damaged Vehicle
2026 Tesla Model X
Damage
Left side mirror destroyed — $2,367 est. repair
Post-Incident
Bus did not stop (hit-and-run)
What happened: While traveling eastbound through the Holland Tunnel, a NJ Transit bus (Route 163, Bus #22054) in the adjacent left lane had an unsecured luggage bay door that was open and protruding into the neighboring lane. As the bus passed alongside the Tesla Model X, the open bay door struck and destroyed the vehicle's left side mirror. The force of the impact swung the bay door shut. The bus did not stop after the collision. The entire incident was captured on the Tesla's built-in dashcam system from four simultaneous camera angles.

Key Findings

  1. The Tesla Model X was traveling in its lane in a normal, straight path through the Holland Tunnel. No lane change or evasive maneuver was made by the Tesla driver.
  2. The NJ Transit bus (Route 163, #22054) had an unsecured luggage bay door that was open and protruding into the adjacent lane, creating a hazard for vehicles in that lane.
  3. The open bay door struck and destroyed the Tesla's left side mirror as the bus passed alongside. The force of the impact swung the bay door shut.
  4. The bus did not stop after the collision, constituting a hit-and-run.
  5. Four simultaneous camera angles from the Tesla's dashcam system provide comprehensive, unambiguous documentation of the incident, the at-fault vehicle's identity (Route 163, Bus #22054), and the resulting damage.
